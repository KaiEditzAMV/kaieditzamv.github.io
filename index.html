About Me

I’m Kai, an anime video editor who loves crafting smooth transitions and dynamic edits. I mainly edit with CapCut but will be switching to After Effects soon. Anime and editing are my biggest passions—I’m always looking for ways to improve and take my work to the next level. When I’m not editing, I’m probably enjoying some Alfredo or hanging out in mischievous acts.

My Editing Style

I create anime music videos (AMVs) that match the vibe of the anime with smooth transitions and effects. I also do character edits, highlighting specific characters and their best moments. I love reworking classic anime scenes with clean effects and pacing, and I focus on smooth, impactful transitions that keep the flow. Additionally, I explore different anime themes, capturing their essence visually in my edits.
